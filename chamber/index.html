Leslie Murkel graduated from Manchester High school over the summer. THe Knight Alumni will attend Virginia Tech in the fall of 2023. "I know many students go straight to college in the fall following graduation. But I want to just get into the world first. I want to gain experience into what the full-time work force is like. I want to work and save up so I don't have to heavily rely on student loans. During this time, I also hope to see what I do and don't like and determine from those experiences what it is I actually want to do with my life and study it in college."

On September 9th, Kevin Rank returned home from serving a 2 year tour over seas. This was his 3rd tour while in the Army. Kevin first inlisted soon after the attacks of 9/11. After serving in the Army for 21 years, he plans to retire at the end of this year. During an interview, he had this to say about his experience. "During my time of service, I had my highs and I had my lows. I met a lot of great people over the years. But I also lost good friends too. When you've seen as much as a soldier has seen, it can either make or break you. It can lead you down a dark path. Or you could get up and do something about it. Get help if you need it or help someone who needs help. The point is is that you can either let the bad things that happen affect you and others or you could act for yourself and do something good for a change. As for myself, I choose the latter."
