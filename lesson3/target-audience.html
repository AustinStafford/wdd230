Description of Target Audience

The target audience is the average citizen, especially of the younger generation, who is community and business oriented. Our goal is to help and assit the average citizen within the community and loccal businesses through politics, education, advocacy, and building relationships.

Two Personas

Five Scenarios

  • Researching opportunities within the community related to business such as jobs or partnerships.
  • Looking up local events to participate in such as building partnerships or advocating.
  • Seeking membership in the chamber of commerce for better opportunities.
  • Researching for ways to help the community grow and improve.
  • Seeking to grow business within the community.
