Description of Target Audience

The target audience is the average citizen, especially of the younger generation, who is community and business oriented. Our goal is to help and assit the average citizen within the community and loccal businesses through politics, education, advocacy, and building relationships.

Two Personas

Why the average citizen? The average citizen is concerned with the well being of thier community and how local business can affect it. They understand the potential the two can have in helping the other grow and improve. They know that this is done through politics, education, advocating, and building relationships.

Why specifically the younger generation? The younger generation is the future of the community and business, both present and future. It is the older generation's responsibility to teach this responsibility to the younger generation so they will be prepared and know how they can best help the community and businesses grow and improve.

Five Scenarios

  • Researching opportunities within the community related to business such as jobs or partnerships.
  • Looking up local events to participate in such as building partnerships or advocating.
  • Seeking membership in the chamber of commerce for better opportunities.
  • Researching for ways to help the community grow and improve.
  • Seeking to grow business within the community.
