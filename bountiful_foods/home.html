Summary Information

From scenic ocean views to beautiful flower fields, Carlsbad is a beautiful coastal city in California. This coastal city is home to many tourist attractions. Its sandy beaches include Tamarack beach which is known for it's surf waves, South Carlsbad State Beach known as a great camping destination, and Carlsbad Lagoon known mainly for recreational water activities. In another part of Carlsbad is LEGOLAND, an amusment park based off of LEGOS. Last but not least, Carlsbad, California is the home of our very own Bountiful Foods!
