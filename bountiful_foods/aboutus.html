History

Bountiful Foods is a small business that specializes in organic, locally grown foods. We are physically located in Carlsbad, California. With that being said, we do have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. The company sells fruits and produce directly from the farmers in the area, while also using some national and international suppliers. Our owner-operated company caters to walk in traffic at our storefront in Carlsbad and, of course, our many food trucks across the coastal area. We deliver and manage orders on the phone and via our website for a more convenient experience. Our mission is to support healthy eating by providing fresh and healthy food options in a convenient way. This includes organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, for the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas.
